IDA PRO

**Shortcuts**

    <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kdb>Enter</kdb> Next window.
    <br>
    <kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>F12</kbd> Show strings window.
    <br>
    <kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>F12</kbd> Show strings window.
    <br>
    <kbd>Alt</kbd>+<kbd>T</kbd> Full text search.

    <kbd>;</kbd> Enter comment.
